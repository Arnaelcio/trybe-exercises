Recipe #1

  • Time: 3 min_
  • Serves: 1 persons
Ingredients Quantity
oil 1 tbsp
Eggs 1

how to do:
To make a fried egg with the yolk well, you should put enough oil and butter in a frying pan, heating over medium heat. Place the egg in the frying pan and, with the help of a spoon, throw the fat on top of the egg white. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
